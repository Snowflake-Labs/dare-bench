This dataset contains daily weather data for major cities in India from January 1, 2000 to December 31, 2024. It is designed for use in climate analysis, weather trend studies, and machine learning applications such as regression, forecasting, and time-series modeling.

It includes both standard and apparent (feels-like) temperatures, along with various meteorological measurements.

---

### 🌦️ Features Included:

- **city** – Name of the Indian city
- **date** – Daily timestamp (YYYY-MM-DD)
- **temperature_2m_max (°C)** – Maximum air temperature at 2 meters height
- **temperature_2m_min (°C)** – Minimum air temperature at 2 meters height
- **apparent_temperature_max (°C)** – Feels-like max temperature considering humidity & wind
- **apparent_temperature_min (°C)** – Feels-like min temperature
- **precipitation_sum (mm)** – Total daily precipitation
- **rain_sum (mm)** – Total daily rainfall
- **weather_code** – WMO weather interpretation code
- **wind_speed_10m_max (km/h)** – Max wind speed at 10m height
- **wind_gusts_10m_max (km/h)** – Max wind gusts at 10m
- **wind_direction_10m_dominant (°)** – Dominant wind direction in degrees

=== About this file ===

About this file
A cleaned CSV file containing daily weather data for 10 major Indian cities from 2000 to 2024.
Rows: ~91,000+ (10 cities × ~9,131 days)
Columns: 12
Format: UTF-8, comma-separated
Date range: 2000-01-01 to 2024-12-31
Ready for time-series, regression, and forecasting tasks


=== Columns & descriptions ===

city: Name of the Indian city where the weather data was recorded
date: Date of the observation in YYYY-MM-DD format
temperature_2m_max: Maximum air temperature at 2 meters above ground
temperature_2m_min: Minimum air temperature at 2 meters above ground
apparent_temperature_max: Maximum feels-like temperature (adjusted for humidity and wind)
apparent_temperature_min: Minimum feels-like temperature
precipitation_sum: Total daily precipitation including all forms (rain, snow, etc.)
rain_sum: Total daily rainfall (liquid precipitation only)
weather_code: Encoded weather condition based on WMO standards
wind_speed_10m_max: Maximum wind speed measured at 10 meters above ground
wind_gusts_10m_max: Maximum wind gusts measured at 10 meters
wind_direction_10m_dominant: Dominant wind direction (in degrees from north) at 10 meters
