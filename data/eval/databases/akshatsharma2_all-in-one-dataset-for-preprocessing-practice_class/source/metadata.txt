# Description of columns:

1) name (Categorical) – Contains fictional character names such as Sherlock, Holmes, Watson, Moriarty (from Sherlock Holmes) and Castiel, Sam, Dean, Bobby (from Supernatural), added to make the dataset fun and engaging.😉

2) city (Nominal) – Includes fictional locations like Gotham, Wakanda, Asgard, Purgatory from the DCU and MCU universes.

3) gender (Nominal) – Indicates gender as Male or Female.

4) profession (Ordinal) – Represents educational qualification in increasing order: Bachelor, Master, PhD.

5) age (Numerical) – Age of the individual in years.

6) cgpa (Numerical) – Cumulative Grade Point Average on a given scale.

7) placed (Label) – Placement status of the individual.
