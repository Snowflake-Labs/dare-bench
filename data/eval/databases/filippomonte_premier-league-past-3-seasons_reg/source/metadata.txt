Match records from the 2022-2025 season, including matches that have not occurred yet. I included a link for each match to find additional information on it. The dataset is meant to be used in educational purposes, for programmers to create and test predictive algorithms. It's still a work in progress... 

=== About this file ===

About this file
This is a dataset containing records of all premier league matches since 2022/2023 season, plus uncompleted matches for the 2024/2025 season.


=== Columns & descriptions ===

Matchday: Game's matchday for that season
Date: Date of match
Time: Time match started in UK time
Home Team: Home team for that game
homeScore: Amount of goals scored by home team
homeXG: Expected goals for home team
awayScore: Amount of goals scored by away team
awayXG: Expected goals for away team
Away Team: Away team for that match
Attendance: Amount of people attending stadium of match
Referee: [no description]
Stadium: [no description]
Result: [no description]
*Additional Stats: [no description]
