**Description:**
This dataset contains the monthly passenger traffic in Sri Lanka from January 2011 to December 2023. It includes the total number of passengers (arrivals and departures combined) for each month. The data from 2014 to 2023 were extracted from official performance reports of the Department of Immigration & Emigration of Sri Lanka. The data from 2011 to 2013 were generated using AI-based extrapolation to maintain continuity.  

**Columns:  **
- `Month`: The year and month in YYYY-MM format (e.g., "2011-01" for January 2011).  
- `Passengers`: The total number of passengers recorded for that month (arrivals + departures).  

**Source: **
- Data (2014–2023) from official reports of the Department of Immigration & Emigration of Sri Lanka.  
- AI-generated data (2011–2013) for consistency and trend continuity.  

**Usage:**
This dataset is valuable for time series forecasting, tourism trend analysis, economic research, and machine learning applications.
