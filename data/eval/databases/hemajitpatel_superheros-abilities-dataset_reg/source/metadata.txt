This dataset features 200 fictional superhero and villain characters drawn from Marvel and DC universes. Each entry includes stats such as strength, speed, intelligence, combat skill, and overall power. It’s a clean, beginner-friendly dataset with some intentionally missing values to simulate real-world data issues.

Great for:

Classification (e.g., predicting alignment)

Regression (e.g., predicting power score)

Clustering (e.g., grouping heroes by attributes)

EDA & Visualization practice

=== About this file ===

About this file
The dataset consists of 1 CSV file:
superhero_abilities_dataset.csv
It includes 200 rows × 10 columns, representing stats and attributes for fictional superheroes and villains. The data is randomly generated and suitable for model training, analysis, or visualization.
Note: This dataset is initially for beginners so the size of data is minimal, I will update it based on feedback or feature extensions.


=== Columns & descriptions ===

Name: Unique name of the superhero/villain (fictional characters)
Universe: Unique name of the superhero/villain (fictional characters)
Alignment: Moral alignment: Hero, Villain, or Anti-Hero
Strength: Physical power level (1 to 10; may have missing values)
Speed: Movement/combat speed (1 to 10; may have missing values)
Intelligence: Strategic thinking and intellect (1 to 10; may have missing values)
Combat Skill: Fighting proficiency and martial skill (1 to 10)
Weapon: Primary weapon or power type (e.g., Tech, Magic, Sword)
Power Score: Overall calculated power rating (40–100)
Popularity Score: Fictional public popularity rating (1–100)
