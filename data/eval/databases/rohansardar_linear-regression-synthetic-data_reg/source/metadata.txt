This dataset contains synthetically generated data intended for linear regression modeling. It simulates a linear relationship defined by the equation:
$$
y=4.5x+1+noise 
$$

- Number of data points: 230 (200 regular + 30 outliers)
- Noise: Gaussian noise with standard deviation of 30
- Outliers: 30 points intentionally added with large deviations to simulate subtle anomalies in the data

This dataset is ideal for practicing linear regression, outlier detection, and robust modeling techniques.
