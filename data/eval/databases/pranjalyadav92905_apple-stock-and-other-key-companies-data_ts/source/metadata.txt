=== About this file ===

About this file
This dataset contains historical stock price data for Apple Inc. (AAPL), collected from publicly available sources such as Yahoo Finance. It includes daily trading information such as open and close prices, trading volume, and adjusted closing prices. This data is often used in financial analysis, trend detection, and machine learning projects for time series forecasting or investment modeling.


=== Columns & descriptions ===

Date: The trading date in YYYY-MM-DD format
Open: Stock price at the beginning of the trading day
High: Highest stock price during the trading day
Low: Lowest stock price during the trading day
Close: Stock price at market close on that trading day
Adj Close: Closing price adjusted for dividends and stock splits
Volume: Total number of shares traded on that particular day
