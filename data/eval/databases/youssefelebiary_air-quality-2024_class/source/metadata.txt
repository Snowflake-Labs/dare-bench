# Context
Air pollution causes approximately **7 million premature deaths annually** (WHO). This dataset enables researchers and data scientists to:
- Analyze global pollution disparities
- Investigate health impacts of air quality
- Develop predictive models for environmental monitoring

# Overview
| Category        | Detail                          |
|-----------------|---------------------------------|
| Records         | 52,000+ daily measurements     |
| Timeframe       | January - December 2024 GMT Time Zone       |
| Cities          | 6 globally distributed locations|
| Pollutants      | CO, CO₂, NO₂, SO₂, O₃, PM2.5, PM10 |
| Index           | European AQI                   |

# Files
1. **Combined Dataset** (`Air_Quality.csv`)
   - All cities with `City` identifier
   - Complete 2024 daily records
   
2. **City-Specific Files** (e.g., `London_Air_Quality.csv`)
   - Same metrics without `City` column
   - Ideal for localized analysis

# Key Features
1. Temporal Coverage: Daily data for all of 2024.
2. Geographical Diversity: Includes cities from all inhabited continents (e.g., "London, Delhi, Sydney").
3. Standardized Units: Explicitly documented (CO₂ in ppm, others in µg/m³).
4. European AQI: Consistent index for cross-city comparison.

# Uses
This dataset is suitable for a wide range of tasks, including:
- Time-series forecasting: Predict AQI based on pollutant levels.
- Pollutant correlation analysis: Identify which gases/particulates most impact AQI.
- Geospatial comparisons: Compare air quality trends across continents.
- Health impact studies: Link PM2.5/PM10 levels to public health benchmarks (WHO guidelines).

=== About this file ===

About this file
Contains over 52,000 entries representing the air quality of the 6 cities during 2024


=== Columns & descriptions ===

Date: Date of the reading
City: City of reading
CO: Carbon Monoxide Concentration in µg/m³
CO2: Carbon Dioxide Concentration in ppm
NO2: Nitrogen Dioxide Concentration in µg/m³
SO2: Sulphur Dioxide Concentration in µg/m³
O3: Ozone Concentration in µg/m³
PM2.5: Particulate Matter 2.5 Concentration in µg/m³
PM10: Particulate Matter 10 Concentration in µg/m³
AQI: European Air Quality Index
