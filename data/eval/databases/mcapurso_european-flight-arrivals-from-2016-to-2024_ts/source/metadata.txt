The dataset is the result of the notebook Beyond the Surface: Forecasting 2025 AirTraffic , from data coming from the dataset made available by Eurocontrol on its Aviation Intelligence Portal at [https://ansperformance.eu] . It summarise **total flight arrivals** in European airports , day by day, for the period 2016-204 .
