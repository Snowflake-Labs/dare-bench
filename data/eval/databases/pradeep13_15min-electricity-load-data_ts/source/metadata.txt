This data has 15 minute interval simulated thermal power-station generated electricity load data for a particular grid from Indian Power Generation Company. This data mainly serves for forecasting playground to achieve greater ac-curacies (+-5%).
1) Day_Interval - "%d-%b-%Y %H:%M" format for every 15 Minute Interval.
2) Frequency_in_Hz - Electricity generated measured in Hz frequency.
3) Penalty for under generation in Indian Rupees.
