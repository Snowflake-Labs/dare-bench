This dataset contains monthly household energy consumption records from 1991 to 2000, enriched with features such as average temperature, Cooling and Heating Degree Days (CDD & HDD), property size, and equipment changes (pump/meter). The data is cleaned and structured to support seasonal analysis, electricity bill simulation, and predictive modeling using techniques like SARIMA and XGBoost.

Perfect for:

Forecasting energy usage and utility costs

Exploring seasonal patterns and climate impact on consumption

Conducting anomaly detection and evaluating energy efficiency

Building machine learning pipelines with real-world utility data

Ideal for data practitioners, energy researchers, and freelancers seeking to showcase forecasting and optimization skills with authentic time series data.

=== About this file ===

About this file
This dataset contains monthly household energy usage records from January 1991 to December 2000, designed for forecasting and modeling tasks like SARIMA and XGBoost.


=== Columns & descriptions ===

NUM: Observation number
YEAR: Year
MONTH: Month
BILL: Amount of bill (in dollars), includes 5% sales tax
TEMP: Average temperature (in degrees Fahrenheit)
HDD: Heating Degree Days
CDD: Cooling Degree Days
SIZE: Number of family members at home
METER: New meter? (indicator variable, 1 = yes)
PUMP1: New heat pump 1? (indicator variable, 1= new)
PUMP2: New heat pump 2? (indicator variable, 1= new)
RIDER TOTAL: Total charge (per kwh) for all riders
CONSUMPTION: Calculated consumption (in kwh)
date: [no description]
BILL_CONSUMPTION_DEVIATION: [no description]
ESTIMATED_TIERED_BILL: [no description]
ENERGY_EFFICIENCY_PER_OCCUPANT: [no description]
