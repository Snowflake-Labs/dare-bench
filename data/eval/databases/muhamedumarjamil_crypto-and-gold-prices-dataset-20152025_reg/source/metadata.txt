This dataset contains simulated daily closing prices in USD for major cryptocurrencies — Bitcoin (BTC), Ethereum (ETH), Cardano (ADA), Binance Coin (BNB), Ripple (XRP), Dogecoin (DOGE), Solana (SOL) — along with gold prices per ounce. The data spans from January 1, 2015, through July 24, 2025. It is intended for research, analysis, and modeling purposes where real historical price patterns are approximated to simulate market behavior over time.

=== About this file ===

About this file
This CSV file contains daily simulated price data from January 1, 2015, to July 24, 2025. Each row represents a single date with corresponding closing prices (in USD) for seven major cryptocurrencies and gold. The dataset is ideal for testing trading strategies, performing time-series analysis, or educational purposes where real market data is not required.


=== Columns & descriptions ===

Date: Trading date
Bitcoin (USD): BTC price
Ethereum (USD): ETH price
Gold (USD per oz): Gold price
Cardano (ADA): ADA price
Binance Coin (BNB): BNB price
Ripple (XRP): XRP price
Dogecoin (DOGE): DOGE price
Solana (SOL): SOL price
