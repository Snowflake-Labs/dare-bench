The Dataset of apple stocks has four features:
- Data
- Close Price
-  High Price
- Low Price
- Open Price
- Volume

The data is ideal for financial analysis, trend forecasting, and correlation studies between Apple and its key partners in the tech industry.

=== About this file ===

About this file
This .csv file contains apple stock data from 2010 to 2024 and have 6 columns


=== Columns & descriptions ===

Date: The timestamp of the stock records in DD-MM-YYYY. Captures day-level trading data.
Close Price: Close price of the stock on each day
High Price: Highest price of the stock reached during the day
Low Price: Lowest price of the stock reached during the day
Open Price: Open Price of the stock on that day
Volume: Total volume of the stock on that day
