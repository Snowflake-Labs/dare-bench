This dataset simulates **procurement and supply chain** data for strategic decision-making using the **Kraljic Matrix**, a framework to classify products/services based on supply risk and profit impact. It includes realistic biases and patterns to mimic real-world procurement scenarios, such as supplier dependencies, geopolitical risks, and sustainability challenges.


Dataset Details

- Instances: 1,000 synthetic procurement items.
- Features: 11 columns (mix of categorical, numerical, and ordinal data).
- File: realistic_kraljic_dataset.csv

## Key Features
Real-World Biases:

- Strategic Items (e.g., lithium batteries) have high costs, long lead times, and high single-source risk.
- Bottleneck Items (e.g., rare earth metals) are concentrated in limited regions with high supply risk.
- Leverage Items (e.g., steel beams) have high order volumes and low supply risk.
- Non-Critical Items (e.g., office supplies) have low costs and high order volumes.

### Risk Scores:

- Scores (1–5) for supply risk, profit impact, and environmental impact are calibrated to reflect real procurement challenges.

### Geopolitical Clustering:

- Strategic items are often sourced from geopolitically volatile regions (e.g., Asia, Africa).

## Potential Use Cases

- Segmentation Analysis: Classify products into Kraljic quadrants.
- Risk Assessment: Identify high-risk items (e.g., single-source dependencies).
- Sustainability Analysis: Correlate environmental impact with supply risk.
- Predictive Modeling: Train models to auto-classify procurement items.

=== About this file ===

About this file
A synthetic procurement dataset (1000 entries) for Kraljic Matrix analysis, featuring supply risk, profit impact, cost, environmental scores, and supplier dependencies. Includes quadrant classifications (Strategic, Bottleneck, Leverage, Non-Critical) in CSV format.


=== Columns & descriptions ===

Product_ID: Unique identifier for the product.
Product_Name: Name of the product (e.g., lithium batteries, office supplies).
Supplier_Region: Geographic region of the supplier.
Lead_Time_Days: Time taken to deliver the product (in days).
Order_Volume_Units: Annual units purchased.
Cost_per_Unit: Price per unit in USD.
Supply_Risk_Score: Risk of supply disruption (1=Low, 5=High).
Profit_Impact_Score: Impact on profitability (1=Low, 5=High).
Environmental_Impact: Sustainability risk (1=Low, 5=High).
Single_Source_Risk: Dependency on a single supplier (Yes/No).
Kraljic_Category: Quadrant classification: Strategic, Bottleneck, Leverage, Non-Critical.
