I created this dataset to explore how lifestyle and environmental factors influence human productivity, measured as a **"Focus score"** on a scale from **0 to 100**. The data was generated using logic-driven simulation techniques to mimic realistic patterns based on sleep, caffeine intake, environmental conditions, and work habits.

This dataset is ideal for practicing **regression modeling, feature engineering, and end-to-end ML pipelines**. Feature correlations are intentionally embedded to help learners understand how structured inputs can drive model performance.

About this file
Format: CSV (Encoding: UTF-8)
Rows: 10,000
Columns: 6 (including target focus_score)
Missing Values:  Present


=== Columns & descriptions ===

hours_slept: Total hours slept in the last 24 hours (range: 3–10)
caffeine_mg: Total caffeine intake in mg (range: 0–500)
noise_level_db: Ambient noise level (in decibels)
task_type: Type of work: Creative, Analytical, Routine, Managerial
work_environment: Where work happens: Home, Office, Cafe, Co-working
focus_score: Final focus/productivity score (0–100)
