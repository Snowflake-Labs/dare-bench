This dataset, generated by ChatGPT, contains over 100,000(1 min intervals) randomly created records designed to simulate the operational data of a centrifugal pump. It includes the following columns:

**Timestamp:** Time of the recorded data.

**Vibration:** Vibration levels of the pump. (mm/s)

**Temperature:** Operating temperature of the pump. (°C)

**Flow Rate:** Rate of fluid flow through the pump. (L/min)

**Pressure:** Pressure levels within the pump system. (bar)

**Power Consumption:** Energy consumed by the pump during operation. (kW)

**Failure:** Binary indicator (0 = normal operation, 1 = failure) to denote the pump's health status.

The dataset incorporates realistic variations and anomalies to mimic real-world scenarios, making it suitable for predictive maintenance projects, particularly for predicting the health and failure of centrifugal pumps.

If you have access to a similar dataset with real-world data (preferably free), please feel free to reach out. Your contribution would be greatly appreciated for advancing this project.
