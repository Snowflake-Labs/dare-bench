This dataset contains 5 years of simulated delivery data for several customers in several different locations. The purpose of this dataset is to mimic real-world delivery patters by including seasonality, demand spikes, and holiday closures. This dataset is perfect for anyone trying to practice time-series forecasting, demand planning, or supply chain analysis.

=== About this file ===

About this file
5 years of synthetic delivery data for several customers and locations


=== Columns & descriptions ===

WorkDate: Date of delivery
Customer: Customer serviced
Location: Hub location where orders originated from
BusinessType: Type of delivery done
OrderCount: Number of orders
NumberOfPieces: Number of pieces
TotalRevenue: Total revenue collected from deliveries
