This data comes from a panel of 4360 observations from 1980 to 1987. It includes information about a worker's type of job, residence area, hourly wage, and so on.


=== About this file ===

About this file
Information about workers, their wage and other relevant features


=== Columns & descriptions ===

rownames: [no description]
nr: identifier
year: [no description]
school: years of schooling
exper: years of experience (=age-6-school)
union: wage set by collective bargaining?
ethn: ethnicity (black, hisp, other)
maried: married?
health: health issues?
wage: Log of hourly wage
industry: a factor with 12 levels
occupation: a factor with 9 levels
residence: a factor with levels (rural area, north east, northern central, south)
