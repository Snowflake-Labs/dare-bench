Overview
The Medicare & Medicaid Electronic Health Record (EHR) Incentive Programs Counts dataset tracks the number of eligible health care providers who have attested to using a certified EHR product under the Medicare EHR Incentive Program. It covers two major provider settings—office-based (ambulatory) practitioners and non-federal acute-care hospitals—and reports counts by developer/vendor product.

Coverage & Granularity

Time span: Program Years 2011 through 2016 (most recent available).

Provider types:

Office-based health care providers (ambulatory)

Non-federal acute-care hospitals (inpatient)

Dimensions:

Developer/Vendor – name of the EHR product developer

ProgramYear – reporting year (filterable via API)

ProviderType – ambulatory vs. inpatient

ProviderCount – number of providers attesting

=== About this file ===

About this file
developer
Type: String
What it is: The name of the certified EHR (electronic health record) vendor providers report using when attesting under the Medicare EHR Incentive Program.
Why it matters: Allows you to group and compare usage by vendor. For a full list of CEHRT-certified vendors, see the ONC Certified Health IT Products List (CHPL).
provider_type
Type: Categorical (professional | hospital)
What it is: Indicates whether the reporting entity is an office-based clinician (“professional”) or a non-federal acute-care hospital (“hospital”).
Why it matters: Enables analysis of adoption patterns separately for individual clinicians versus hospitals.
program_year
Type: Integer (2011 – 2016)
What it is: The Medicare EHR Incentive Program year in which the attestation occurs. Totals for each year are cumulative up through that year.
Why it matters: Tracks growth over time in provider participation and technology uptake.
tot_provs_report_developer
Type: Integer
What it is: The total count of providers (of that provider_type, through the given program_year) who report using any certified product from that developer.
Why it matters: Measures the overall market share of each vendor.
tot_provs_report_2015_edition
Type: Integer
What it is: How many of those providers reporting a product by that developer are on the 2015 Edition certification?
Why it matters: Shows uptake of the most recent certification standard (2015 Edition).
tot_provs_report_2014_edition
Type: Integer
What it is: How many of those providers reporting a product by that developer are on the 2014 Edition certification?
Why it matters: Tracks continued use of the prior certification standard.
tot_provs_report_2011_edition
Type: Integer
What it is: How many of those providers reporting a product by that developer are on the original 2011 Edition certification?
Why it matters: Indicates how many providers have not yet upgraded beyond the initial standard.
product_type
Type: Categorical (commercial | self-developed)
What it is: Classifies each EHR product as either commercially marketed (sold broadly) or self-developed (created in-house for exclusive use by that organization).
Why it matters: Differentiates widespread commercial solutions from bespoke, internally built systems.
