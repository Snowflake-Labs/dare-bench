## Algerian Forst Fires Dataset

Data Set information

The dataset inclueds 244 instances that regroup a data of two regions of Algeria, namely Bejaia region location in the northeast of Algeria and the Sidi Bel-abbes region located in the northwest of Algeria.

122 instances for each region

The period from June 2012 to September 2012. The dataset includes 11 attributes and 1 output attribute (class). The 244 instances have been classified into fire(138 cases) and not fire (106) classes.

Attribute Information:
- Date: (DD/MM/YYYY) Day, month, year weather data infomation
- Temp; temperature noon in Celcius degree
- RH: Relative Humidity in %
- Ws: Wind Speed
- Rain: total day in mm
- Fine Fuel Moisture Code(FFMC): index from the FWI system
- Duff Moisture Code (DMC) index from FWI system
- Drought Code (DC) index from FWI system
- Initial Spread Index (ISI) from the FWI system
- Buildup Index (BUI) from the FWI system
- Fire Weather Index (FWI) 
- Classes Fire, Not Fire

=== Columns & descriptions ===

day: day
month: from July to August
year: year 2012
Temperature: Temperature at noon in C
RH: Relative humidity in %
Ws: Wind speed in Km/h
Rain: Amount of rainfall in mm
FFMC: Fine Fuel Moisture Code - It tells us how dry the surface layer of forest or grassland fuels are, and therefore how easily they might ignite and spread fire
DMC: Duff Moisture Code - It is a numeric rating of the average moisture content of loosely compacted organic layers of moderate depth.
DC: Drought Code - It is a numeric rating of the average moisture content of deep, compact organic layers
ISI: Initial Spread Index - It  is a numeric rating of the expected rate of fire spread. It is based on wind speed and FFMC
BUI: Buildup Index - It is a numeric rating of the total amount of fuel available for combustion. It is based on the DMC and the DC
FWI: Fire Weather Index - It is a numeric rating of fire intensity. It is based on the ISI and the BUI, and is used as a general index of fire danger throughout the forested areas of Canada.
Classes: Classes of Fire and Not Fire
