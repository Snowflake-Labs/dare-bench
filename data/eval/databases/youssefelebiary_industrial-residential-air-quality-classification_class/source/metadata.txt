# Context
Air pollution is a global concern, contributing to **7 million premature deaths annually** (WHO). This dataset enables researchers and data scientists to:
- Analyze air quality differences between industrial and residential areas
- Develop classification models to predict area types based on pollution levels
- Investigate environmental impacts of urban zoning

# Overview
| Category        | Detail                          |
|-----------------|---------------------------------|
| Records         | 10,000+ daily measurements     |
| Timeframe       | January - December 2024 GMT Time Zone       |
| Locations       | Moscow, Delhi, Beijing, Zurich, Vancouver, Stockholm   |
| Pollutants      | CO, NO₂, SO₂, O₃, PM2.5, PM10  |
| Classification  | Industrial or Residential labels |

# Files
* `City_Types.csv`
   - All locations with `Location` and `Classification` (Industrial/Residential) identifiers
   - Complete 2024 daily records

# Key Features
1. **Temporal Coverage**: Daily data for all of 2024.
2. **Classification Labels**: Each record labeled as "Industrial" or "Residential" for machine learning tasks.
3. **Geographical Diversity**: Includes urban areas with varied industrial and residential zoning.
4. **Standardized Units**: Explicitly documented (e.g., CO₂ in ppm, others in µg/m³).

# Uses
This dataset is suitable for a wide range of tasks, including:
- **Classification Modeling**: Build models to predict industrial vs. residential areas based on air quality metrics.
- **Pollutant Correlation Analysis**: Identify which pollutants (e.g., PM2.5, NO₂) most distinguish industrial from residential zones.
- **Environmental Impact Studies**: Compare air quality trends between industrial and residential areas.
- **Policy Analysis**: Link pollutant levels to urban planning and zoning regulations.

About this file

=== Columns & descriptions ===

Date: Date of the reading
City: City of reading
CO: Carbon Monoxide Concentration in µg/m³
NO2: Nitrogen Dioxide Concentration in µg/m³
SO2: Sulphur Dioxide Concentration in µg/m³
O3: Ozone Concentration in µg/m³
PM2.5: Particulate Matter 2.5 Concentration in µg/m³
PM10: Particulate Matter 10 Concentration in µg/m³
Type: Type of city
